Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Fried ("the app", "we", "us") is built to respect your privacy by default. This policy explains what we do — and mostly don't — collect.

The short version

Fried collects no personal data and requires no account. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

Information we collect

  • None that identifies you. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, or location, and we have no servers that receive your data.
  • On-device data only. Your quiz answers, reaction-game results, Fried Score, history, and plan progress are stored locally on your device (in the app's storage) so the app can show your trend and streak. This never leaves your phone, and is deleted if you delete the app.

Apple Intelligence

If your device supports it, your roast and analysis are generated by Apple's on-device foundation models. This processing happens entirely on your device; your inputs are not sent to us or to any server.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple's handling of that transaction is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Notifications

If you enable the optional daily reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. We do not send push notifications from a server and collect nothing in connection with reminders.

Analytics & tracking

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising, or tracking of any kind. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

Fried is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@fried.app.
