## TOP 5 WINNING FORMATS (ranked for Fried)

### 1. The Score Reveal — "the number IS the hook" (faceless) ⭐ highest fit
**Evidence:** Umax PSL score (1B impressions), Mental Age (27M), Senity %, Cal AI.
**Structure:** curiosity hook in first 2 sec → run the test on screen → **flash the absurd number/verdict** → "go get yours."
**Fried script:** On-screen text *"This app said my brain is 47. I'm 19."* → screen-record the 90-sec test sped up → the **Brain Age / Fried Score / archetype card** snaps in. The card is the creative.
**Sub-units (highest spread):** the *shocked-reaction duet* ("it said I'm Deep Fried 💀") and the *stitch* template ("I'm 19 but my brain age is 47").

### 2. Scan-a-Stranger / Street Interview — live proof on a real human
**Evidence:** Eyepic 195.4M (the single highest-aggregate format in the study), Cal AI restaurant version.
**Structure:** walk up to a stranger → "let me show you how fried your brain is" → they take it → **react to their score on camera.**
**Fried script:** "Rating strangers' brain rot until someone scores worse than me." Highest authenticity + duet/stitch chains. Your "does it actually work?" proof in one shot.

### 3. The Aesthetic Edit — score as the punchline (UGC fan-edit) — highest FREE ceiling
**Evidence:** @iblamelookss 124K off 12.5K followers, Umax "ascension" edits, @looksmaxxing 45K.
**Structure:** slick montage on bass-heavy synth (Mareux "The Perfect Girl"-type) → the result card flashes mid-edit → app **tagged in caption, never pitched.** Reads as culture, not an ad.
**Fried script:** moody "brain rot" edit (2am scrolling, doom imagery, glitch) → the Fried Score card drops as the punchline → @fried tagged. Post from small "edit" accounts.

### 4. The Native-Trend Listicle — aura-points crossover (text slideshow, comment bait)
**Evidence:** @sunntie aura 26M, Astroscope zodiac 250M+, "GPA before/after."
**Structure:** faceless POV listicle assigning point values/categories to everyday moments → the running score is the payoff → app as the "official scorer." Engineered for comment fights.
**Fried script:** *"Things frying your brain (and the aura you lose): doomscrolling at 2am −10,000… watching a movie at 1.5x −5,000… → take the Fried test for your real score."* Hijacks an existing 26M-view trend.

### 5. Problem-Agitation → App-as-fix (the DR walkthrough) — the PAID scaler
**Evidence:** Unrot "this is brainrot in real-time / earn your dopamine," Wiser "stop scrolling," **Cal AI's scaled paid format** (bold text overlays + fast-cut walkthrough + explicit CTA), Nebula quiz funnel (148 ads).
**Structure:** name the pain viscerally → app = the fix → **explicit CTA** ("take the test") + bold text overlays. This is the version you put **paid spend** behind after organic proves the hook.
**Fried script:** *"Constant scrolling is frying your brain in real-time. Here's exactly how fried you are — and how to fix it."* → fast app walkthrough → "Take the Fried test."

## The rules that separate winners from flops (from the paid teardown of 500+ creatives)
- **Hook in the first 0–2 sec, on-screen text** (must work sound-off, especially on Reels).
- **Declarative hooks beat question hooks** — "?" hooks had only **8% survival**. ("Your brain is 47." not "How fried are you?")
- **Length: under 20s OR 30–60s.** The 20–30s middle dies.
- **Faceless wins on TikTok** (1.5M avg vs 1.3M face-led) and is infinitely cloneable.
- **Volume > polish:** run **10–15 variants**, kill in 3–5 days, scale anything alive at 30 days.
- **Organic finds the winner; paid (a DR re-cut) scales it.** Never run paid on an unproven creative. "Influencer content earns attention; ad content converts — different jobs." (Cal AI)
- **The result card is the unit of distribution** — make Fried's one-tap share card screenshot/duet-bait (it already is: the archetype card).
